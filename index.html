What is FGO?

Fate Gand order is an online, free to play Japanese role-playing game mobile game that was released in Japan in 2015. The game was then brought to North America and Europe in 2017. The game is based on the anime franchise and light novel series of Fate or Fate Stay Night.

The game is centered around turn-based combat where the player (known as the master within the game) controls a wide variety of characters (called servants within the game) to complete different objectives and progress in the story. Servents have different classes giving them different advantages in the game and each character has a unique special attack called a noble phantasm. These servents can be acquired through a system known as "gacha" where you spend saint quarts to potentially roll your desired servent. The player has a party of 6 servents in each battle, 3 active members, and 3 on reserve. Each turn, the player is given a set of 5 command cards and may use 3 to attack. The Cards are categorized as a buster, arts, and crit cards with each having different advantages. Buster cards do more damage, Arts cards help increase your noble phantasm gauge more efficiently, and crit cards generate more crit stars for your servents. The player also has three special abilities called “command spells” that can be used at any time during a battle.
